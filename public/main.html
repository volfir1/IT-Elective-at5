Fun Facts about Serene Places in the Philippines

Fun Fact #1

The Banaue Rice Terraces were built over 2,000 years ago and are considered the "Eighth Wonder of the World."

Fun Fact #2

El Nido, Palawan has been ranked as one of the most beautiful islands in the world, with over 50 white sandy beaches.

Fun Fact #3

The Chocolate Hills in Bohol turn chocolate brown during the dry season, making them resemble giant chocolate mounds.
