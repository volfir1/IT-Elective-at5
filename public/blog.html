Top 5 Hidden Beaches in the Philippines
March 15, 2024 By Maria Santos

The Philippines is home to countless beautiful beaches, but some of the most spectacular ones are hidden from the typical tourist routes. Here are our top 5 picks for secluded beach paradises:

1. Calaguas Islands, Camarines Norte

Crystal clear waters, powdery white sand, and virtually untouched landscapes make this group of islands a true hidden gem...

2. Seco Island, Antique

This remote sandbar offers pristine waters and excellent conditions for kitesurfing...

3. Tambaron Island, Mindoro

A secluded beach with rich marine life and stunning coral formations...

4. Subic Beach, Sorsogon

Known for its pink sand and peaceful atmosphere...

5. Canibad Beach, Samal

A hidden cove with dramatic cliffs and crystal-clear waters...
